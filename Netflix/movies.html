Paava Kadhaigal

Paava Kadhaigal ( transl. Sinful Tales) is a 2020 Indian Tamil -language anthology drama film directed by Sudha Kongara, Gautham Menon, Vetrimaaran and Vignesh Shivan. Through four unique stories, the film explores how pride, honour, and sin influence complex relationships of love.
